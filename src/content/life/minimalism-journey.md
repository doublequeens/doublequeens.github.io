Minimalism isn't just about owning less stuff. It's about being intentional with your time, energy, and attention.

## The Beginning

I started my minimalism journey by clearing out things I didn't need. It was hard at first, but freeing.

## What I Learned

Less isn't just about material possessions. It's about:

- Clearing mental clutter
- Focusing on what matters
- Being present in the moment

## The Result

Today, I feel lighter, more focused, and happier. Minimalism is a practice, not a destination.
